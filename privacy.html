Privacy Policy

We keep this simple. Here's what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

1. Who we are

Devon Marketing Services is a trading name of Screens Normally Ltd, registered in England and Wales (Company No. 09313719). We are the data controller for any personal data you provide to us. You can contact us at hello@devonmarketingservices.com.

2. What data we collect

We only collect information you voluntarily give us. This includes:

  • Your name and email address when you fill in our contact form
  • Any other information you include in your message to us
  • Basic analytics data about how visitors use our website (see section 5)

We do not collect sensitive personal data and we do not ask for payment details through our website.

3. Why we collect it

We use your contact information solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Discuss and deliver the services you've asked about
  • Send you information relevant to your project (invoices, proposals, updates)

We will not add you to a mailing list or contact you for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. How we store and protect your data

Enquiries submitted through our website are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form service. Their privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We store project-related correspondence and files securely. We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for, or as required by law.

5. Website analytics

Our website may use basic analytics to understand how visitors find and use the site (such as which pages are visited and where visitors come from). This data is anonymous and is not linked to any individual. We do not use advertising tracking or retargeting technologies.

6. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies or third-party marketing pixels. You do not need to accept any cookie consent banner to use this site.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Object to us processing your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@devonmarketingservices.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Third-party links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The current version will always be available on this page.
